## Overview

To begin using Osprey Measurement Systems:

1. Unbox your Osprey device.
2. Charge it fully before first use.
3. Download the Osprey mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.
4. Open the app, sign in or create an account.
5. Press the power button on the Osprey device to turn it on.
6. In the app, tap "Add Device".
7. Follow on-screen instructions to pair via Bluetooth.
8. Once connected, you can begin taking measurements.

Need more help? Contact support or visit our community forum.
