## Overview

Follow these steps to quickly set up an Osprey measurement device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Charge your device">
    Plug your Osprey device in and charge until the LED turns green.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Get the official Osprey app from your app store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in or register">
    Open the app, then sign in or register for a free account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair device">
    In the app, tap "Add Device" and follow the prompts to pair via Bluetooth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start measuring">
    Once connected, select your device to begin taking measurements.
  </Step>
</Steps>
