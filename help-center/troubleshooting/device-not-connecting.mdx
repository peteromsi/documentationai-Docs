---
title: Device Not Connecting
description: What to do if your Osprey device won't connect.
---

## Symptoms

- Device not showing up in app
- Bluetooth pairing fails

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check device power">
    Ensure your Osprey device is fully charged and powered on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Bluetooth">
    Turn Bluetooth off/on on your phone. Wait 5 seconds between steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart app">
    Close and reopen the Osprey app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-add device">
    In the app, remove the device and try pairing again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If issues persist, contact support.
